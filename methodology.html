HOW I WORK

Project
Methodology

Every project starts with a problem worth solving and a constraint worth embracing.

The Process

I follow a systematic approach to AI and development projects, with built-in checkpoints for ethical evaluation and technical validation at every stage.

01

Problem Discovery

Identify the real problem, not the perceived one. Talk to actual users, examine data, and challenge assumptions.

02

Ethical Review

Before writing a single line of code, I evaluate potential harms, biases, and accessibility implications.

03

Architecture Design

Choose the right tools, not the newest ones. Design for constraints and future flexibility.

04

Iterative Build

Build small, test constantly, ship early. Every iteration produces a working artifact.

05

Community Testing

Deploy to real users in their environment. Listen to feedback, especially from marginalized groups.

06

Document & Share

Write it all down. Every decision, every trade-off, every lesson. Open-source when possible.

GUIDING PRINCIPLES

Core Values

Autonomy First

Systems should amplify human capability, not replace human agency. AI should help you make better decisions, not make them for you.

Inclusive Design

Accessibility is not a feature — it's a requirement. Every system should work for every body and every mind.

Ethical Standards

Building with AI carries power, and power carries responsibility. I hold myself accountable for the impact of what I create.

Open Knowledge

I share what I learn because knowledge hoarded is knowledge wasted. Open source is a philosophy, not just a license.
