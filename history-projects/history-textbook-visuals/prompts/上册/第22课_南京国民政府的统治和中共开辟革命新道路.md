## 知识点1：南京国民政府成立 —— 宁汉合流（1927）

**英文提示词：** Establishment of Nanjing National Government 1927, Chiang Kai-shek at center during inauguration ceremony, portrait of Sun Yat-sen and party flags behind, government officials flanking, Wuhan government shadow dissolving in lower right corner symbolizing Ning-Han merger, Nanjing government building background, early Republic official photography style, formal solemn atmosphere with undertones of political complexity, dignified top lighting with window daylight, subtle purple shadows hinting at political intrigue, cool gray and blue palette, 8K
