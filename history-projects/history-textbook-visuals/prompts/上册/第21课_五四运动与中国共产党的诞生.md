## 知识点1：巴黎和会外交失败 —— 中国代表交涉（1919）

**视觉风格：** 国际外交场景写实插画，欧洲新古典主义建筑氛围，低沉压抑

**构图：** 巴黎和会会场（凡尔赛宫镜厅），中国代表顾维钧站在发言席上义正词严地陈词，手中拿着关于山东问题的文件。英法美意四国代表冷漠或回避视线。前景桌面上，山东权益被标注转移给日本的地图展开着。画面色调寒冷，体现外交屈辱

**色彩：**
- 主色调：凡尔赛金 #C9B88A、外交黑 #2A2A2A
- 辅色调：冰冷蓝 #4A6A8A、大理石白 #E8E5E0
- 强调色：屈辱红（中国标志） #C41E3A

**光线：** 大吊灯的冷色亮光，人物面部半明半暗，气氛冷凝

**英文提示词：** Paris Peace Conference 1919, Chinese delegate Gu Weijun (Wellington Koo) speaking at the Hall of Mirrors in Versailles Palace, holding document about Shandong question, Western leaders of UK France US Italy looking cold and evasive, map showing Shandong being transferred to Japan on the table, neo-classical diplomatic setting, cold bright chandelier lighting, half-lit faces creating tense atmosphere, historical realist illustration, pale gold and marble tones with icy blue accents, sense of diplomatic humiliation and injustice, 8K

**中文备选：** 顾维钧在巴黎和会据理力争，列强冷漠对待，凡尔赛宫镜厅中的外交屈辱

## 知识点2：五四运动 —— 学生游行示威（1919.5.4）

**视觉风格：** 热血纪实风格插画，新闻摄影式抓拍构图，强烈的运动张力

**构图：** 北京天安门前，数千名学生高举标语横幅涌上街头，横幅上书"外争主权内除国贼""废除二十一条""还我青岛"等字样。画面焦点为一名站在高处的学生领袖振臂高呼，周围学生群情激昂。北大红楼在背景中隐约可见。艳阳高照，白纸黑字的传单在空中飞舞

**色彩：**
- 主色调：运动白 #F5F5F0（标语底）、热血红 #C41E3A（标语字）
- 辅色调：学生装蓝灰 #4A5A6A、天安门红 #7A2A2A
- 强调色：天空蓝 #2A6A9A

**光线：** 高照度白日光，强反差，高饱和，新闻照片般的真实感

**英文提示词：** May Fourth Movement 1919, thousands of students marching in front of Tiananmen Beijing, raising banners reading "Safeguard Sovereignty Expel Traitors", "Abolish Twenty-One Demands", "Return Qingdao", a student leader on a high spot raising arm and shouting, passionate crowd, leaflets flying in air, Peking University Red Building visible in background, photojournalistic style illustration, high-contrast bright sunlight, intense patriotic energy, white banners with bold red characters, documentary realism, chaotic yet determined revolutionary atmosphere, 8K

**中文备选：** 五四运动北京学生天安门游行，标语横幅铺天盖地，热血纪实抓拍

## 知识点3：五四运动 —— 工人罢工商人罢市（1919.6）

**视觉风格：** 社会纪实黑白风格，带有版画般的黑白对比张力

**构图：** 上海南京路场景，商铺全部关门（罢市），门前贴满"抵制日货""不卖国贼"标语。工人游行队伍从街道穿过，身穿工装的工人与身着长衫的商人并肩同行。黄包车夫也加入行列。整个画面以黑白为主色调，只有标语和旗帜的红色突出

**色彩：**
- 主色调：黑白纪实（全饱和度降低）
- 辅色调：罢工黑 #1A1A1A、街道灰 #6A6A6A
- 强调色：罢工红 #C41E3A（只有红色保留高饱和）

**色彩方案：** 类似《辛德勒名单》的彩色点睛效果，整体黑白画面中只有红色保留

**光线：** 阴天的漫射白光，散射光下的人群剪影

**英文提示词：** June 1919 Shanghai general strike, Nanjing Road closed shops with boycott Japanese goods signs, workers and merchants marching together in solidarity, rickshaw pullers joining the procession, black and white illustration with only red color retained on banners and flags, Schindler's List style selective color technique, social documentary aesthetic, woodblock print inspired high-contrast composition, overcast diffused daylight, silent yet powerful protest atmosphere, Chinese working class rising in national movement, 8K

**中文备选：** 上海工人罢工商人罢市，黑白画面中的红色标语，民族觉醒的震撼场景

## 知识点4：五四运动 —— 拒绝在合约上签字（1919.6.28）

**视觉风格：** 胜利喜悦的纪实照片风格，初夏日光暖色调

**构图：** 中国代表团在巴黎愤怒拒签和约后，国内民众奔走相告欢呼庆祝的群像。前景为一名报童高举号外"中国拒签和约！"在街上奔跑。背景群众欢呼、鼓掌、拥抱。北京大学校园内学生们击掌相庆。阳光穿透树叶，洒满光明

**色彩：**
- 主色调：暖阳光 #F5D76E、喜悦红 #C41E3A（号外标题）
- 辅色调：夏日绿 #4A8A5A、民国蓝 #2A5A8A
- 强调色：金色胜利 #F0C040

**光线：** 夏日午后金色阳光穿过树叶，斑驳光影，充满希望和胜利感

**英文提示词：** China refuses to sign Treaty of Versailles June 28 1919, domestic celebration scene, newsboy running with extra edition newspaper screaming "China Refuses!", cheering crowds embracing and applauding, Peking University students celebrating joyfully, warm golden summer afternoon sunlight filtering through tree leaves, dappled light effects, photorealistic illustration style, documentary photograph aesthetic, triumphant and hopeful atmosphere, joy of national victory, bright warm color palette with saturated golds and greens, 8K

**中文备选：** 中国拒签和约消息传来，国内民众欢庆胜利，夏日金色阳光下的喜悦群像

## 知识点5：中共一大 —— 上海李公馆召开（1921.7）

**视觉风格：** 暗夜秘密集会场景，电影化叙事光影，悬疑紧张氛围

**构图：** 上海法租界望志路106号李公馆客厅内，13位代表围坐在长桌周围（毛泽东在其中较为年轻显眼，手持笔记本）。桌上散落文件与茶杯。窗外可见夜色中的法租界街道与巡捕身影移动。室内仅一盏煤油灯照明，代表们的面孔在昏暗中显得专注而坚毅

**色彩：**
- 主色调：夜色黑 #0A0E1A、煤油灯暖黄 #E8C88A
- 辅色调：墙壁米黄 #D4C5A9、窗帘深红 #4A1A2A
- 强调色：灯光聚焦的金色 #F5D76E

**光线：** 单煤油灯作为单一光源，伦勃朗式布光，人脸半明半暗，营造地下秘密会议的氛围

**英文提示词：** First National Congress of the Communist Party of China July 1921, secret meeting in Li Hanjun's residence on Wangzhi Road Shanghai French Concession, 13 delegates seated around a long table, young Mao Zedong among them holding notebook, documents and teacups on table, French Concession street visible through window with patrol shadows passing, single kerosene lamp illuminating the room, Rembrandt lighting on faces, dark cinematic atmosphere, suspenseful clandestine meeting mood, deep shadows with warm golden light on faces, historical film noir aesthetic, 8K

**中文备选：** 中共一大在上海李公馆秘密召开，煤油灯下的历史性聚会，伦勃朗光影

## 知识点6：中共一大 —— 南湖红船（1921.7下旬）

**视觉风格：** 中国水墨画淡彩与革命油画结合，"红船"经典意象

**构图：** 嘉兴南湖湖心，一艘中型画舫静静停泊在湖面上，船头有人放哨。画面透过清晨水面薄雾望向红船，一轮红日初升于东方天际，倒影映在湖面。远处湖岸线的烟雨楼朦胧可见。水面莲叶田田，意境宁静而充满希望

**色彩：**
- 主色调：南湖绿 #3A6A5A、晨雾白 #E8EDE5
- 辅色调：船木深褐 #5A3A2A、朝霞红橙 #E86A3A
- 强调色：红太阳 #D42A1A（低饱和跳跃）

**光线：** 清晨日出时分的柔雾光，逆光勾勒红船轮廓，空气透视感强

**英文提示词：** The Red Boat on South Lake Jiaxing, late July 1921, a medium-sized painted pleasure boat floating quietly on the misty lake at dawn, the first congress reconvening onboard, a sentry watching at the bow, red rising sun on the eastern horizon reflecting on the water, Yanyu Tower faintly visible on distant shore, lotus leaves dotting the lake surface, Chinese ink painting with light color wash blended with revolutionary oil painting style, soft morning mist, backlit boat silhouette, serene yet hopeful atmosphere, classic Chinese revolutionary imagery, 8K

**中文备选：** 嘉兴南湖红船，清晨薄雾中静泊水面，红日初升，水墨淡彩油画风格

**英文提示词：** First National Congress of Kuomintang 1924 Guangzhou, group photograph style historical documentation, Sun Yat-sen standing at podium center, Li Dazhao and Mao Zedong among Communist Party members present, banners reading "Alliance with Russia Alliance with Communists Support Peasants and Workers", delegates in Zhongshan suits and traditional gowns, unified and hopeful atmosphere, panoramic assembly photo composition, archival photograph texture with slight fading, soft diffused indoor lighting, warm beige and gray-blue palette, solemn historical moment of first United Front, 8K
